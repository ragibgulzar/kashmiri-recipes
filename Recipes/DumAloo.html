Dum Aloo


Dum Aloo is a delicious recipe of baby potatoes cooked in a gravy or sauce. This dish is made in various ways in Kashmiri Cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Baby potatoes (boiled and peeled)
  • Oil
  • Kashmiri redchilli powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Jeera
  • Bay leaf
  • Coriander leaves
  • Dried fenugreek leaves
  • Yogurt/curd
  • Salt
