Rogan Josh

A delicious flavourful Lamb curry that comes from Persian origin. It is the signature dish of Kashmir, where the name is derived from Persian words in which Rogan meals oil and Josh mean Intense heat.

Ingredients

  • Mutton
  • Curd
  • Salt
  • Mustard Oil
  • Cardamom
  • Black Pepper Powder
  • Kashmiri red chilli ..
