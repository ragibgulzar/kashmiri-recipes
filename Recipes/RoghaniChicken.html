Roghani Chicken

The roghani chicken comes from the classic rogan josh. Instead of using mutton, this recipe makes use of chicken.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Pieces
  • Lemon Juice
  • Salt
  • Tomatoes and Onion
  • Cashews and Almonds
  • Ginger and Garlic
  • Red chilli Powder and Turmeric Powder
  • Pepper
